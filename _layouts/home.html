Welcome to my website!

I completed my PhD in Economics at Northwestern University in 2026. Starting in July 2026, I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at the University of California, Berkeley.

My research interests are in econometrics, with a focus on network econometrics, causal inference, and statistical decision theory.

In parallel, I am involved in several liver cirrhosis–related projects in collaboration with the Northwestern University Transplant Outcomes Research Collaborative (NUTORC).
